About Us

Your Home in Berakas

Setia Point Hotel is located within Setia Point in the Berakas area of Bandar Seri Begawan, offering guests a convenient and comfortable stay in a well-connected lifestyle destination. Situated on the 6th floor and directly connected to the shopping complex below, the hotel provides easy access to retail outlets, dining options, cafés and daily conveniences all within the same development.

Its strategic location makes it an ideal accommodation choice for locals, travelers, and business guests seeking accessibility, comfort and practicality during their stay. The hotel's modern surroundings and welcoming atmosphere create a relaxing environment suitable for both short and extended visits.

Guests can enjoy seamless connectivity while experiencing the comfort of an integrated hospitality and lifestyle setting. Whether for business, leisure, or family stays, Setia Point Hotel offers a balanced blend of convenience, elegance, and everyday comfort.

Setia Point

Shopping Complex, Hotel & Event Spaces

Setia Point is an integrated lifestyle destination featuring a shopping complex, restaurants, hotel and event hall facilities located in the Berakas area of Bandar Seri Begawan, within the Brunei-Muara District.

Strategically situated along Jalan Berakas, known for its close proximity to Brunei International Airport and a walking distance to Times Square Shopping Centre and Airport Mall — making it a convenient stop for both locals and international visitors.

  • Brunei International Airport nearby
  • Times Square Shopping Centre
  • Airport Mall within walking distance

Featured Suite

Family Suite

The family suite is thoughtfully designed to provide both comfort and functionality, featuring a generously sized living area that creates a welcoming space for relaxation and quality time. The open-concept seamlessly integrates with the living room, offering convenience and a modern, sociable layout ideal for families.

In addition, the suite includes a private balcony allowing guests to enjoy fresh air, natural light, and scenic views in a more exclusive and tranquil setting — perfect for unwinding or casual gatherings.

This bathroom offers a modern, relaxing space designed for comfort and style, featuring warm wood accents, sleek finishes and soft ambient lighting creating a calm atmosphere.

The suite features one ensuite master bedroom and one twin room, thoughtfully designed to provide a warm and comforting ambiance. Each room is styled with soft tones, cozy furnishings and a calming atmosphere, ensuring a restful and relaxing stay for all guests.
