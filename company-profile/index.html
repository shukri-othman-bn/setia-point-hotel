Who We Are

About the Group

Syarikat Mohd Yussof Dan Anak-Anak is a Brunei-owned hospitality and property group with a distinguished portfolio spanning serviced residences, full-service hotels, and integrated lifestyle destinations. Built on family values and a commitment to excellence, the group has evolved from a single property into one of Brunei-Muara's most recognised hospitality names.

We believe exceptional spaces are built on trust, consistency, and attention to detail. Every property under our stewardship is managed to the highest standards — ensuring guests, tenants, and partners benefit from locations that are accessible, well-maintained, and positioned for sustained success.

Commercial Opportunity

Setia Point — A Premier Address for Your Brand

Setia Point is more than a shopping centre — it is an integrated lifestyle destination where retail, hospitality, dining, and events converge. For the right tenant, it offers unmatched visibility, built-in foot traffic, and the backing of an established operator committed to long-term partnership.

Strategic Location

Situated along Jalan Berakas in a high-growth corridor — near Brunei International Airport, Times Square Shopping Centre, and Airport Mall. Ideal for brands seeking convenience and regional visibility.

Integrated Footfall

With an estimated 675,000 annual visitors, foot traffic flows from mall shoppers, 72 hotel rooms on Level 6, and major events at Munawwarah and Mawwaddah Halls on Level 7 — creating a multi-source customer base for retail and F&B tenants.

Professional Stewardship

Managed under Syarikat Mohd Yussof Dan Anak-Anak — a proven operator with a portfolio spanning hospitality and commercial property across Brunei-Muara.

Property 3 · Flagship

Flagship Integrated Lifestyle Destination

Setia Point

Comfort in Every Stay, Hospitality in Every Smile

An integrated lifestyle destination in Berakas — 7,303 m² of retail space, a 72-room hotel, and prestigious event halls across a purpose-built vertical campus. Opened November 2024, Setia Point brings together commerce, hospitality, and celebration under one address.

Building & Destination

  • Retail mall: 7,303 m² · Opened November 2024
  • Levels 1–5: Multi-storey carpark · 540 parking spaces
  • Level 5: Swimming pool (hotel guests)
  • Level 6: Setia Point Hotel · 72 rooms
  • Level 7: Munawwarah Hall (2,400 pax) & Mawwaddah Hall (300 pax)
  • Traffic: Estimated 675,000 annual visitors
  • Nearby: Brunei International Airport, Times Square, Airport Mall

Why Tenants Choose Setia Point

  • Built-in foot traffic from hotel guests, event attendees, and mall visitors
  • Prime Berakas location minutes from Brunei International Airport
  • Owner-operator stewardship under an established hospitality group
  • Integrated ecosystem of retail, dining, hospitality, and events
